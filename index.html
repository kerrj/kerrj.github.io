Justin Kerr

I am a first year PhD student at UC Berkeley in the AUTOLAB working on robot learning, usually for manipulation. Recently I'm interested in NeRFs for grasping, tactile sensing, surgery, and deformables. My work is supported by the NSF GRFP.

Previously I finished my bachelor's at CMU where I worked with Howie Choset on multi-agent path planning, and spent time at Berkshire Grey and NASA's JPL.
